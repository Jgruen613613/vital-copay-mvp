The Process

How It Works

01

Tell Us Who You Are

Our 28-question quiz maps your scent memories, music taste, aesthetic identity, and the emotional territory your fragrance should inhabit.

Not what you think you want — who you actually are.

02

AI Generates Your Brief

Your answers are translated into a precise perfumer's creative brief — olfactive family, specific notes, reference fragrances, and the emotional core.

The bridge between your story and chemistry.

03

A Real Perfumer Creates It

An experienced indie perfumer interprets your brief and creates a genuine EDP at 15–18% concentration. Real fragrance, not a compromise.

Three sample vials shipped to you. Wear each one for a week.

04

We Refine Until It's You

Rate your samples, tell us what works and what doesn't. With each iteration, your fragrance gets closer to something only you could wear.

The feedback loop is the entire product.

About Zoe

The Name Means Life

Zoe was born from a simple observation: scent is the most emotionally powerful sense we have — the only one with a direct neural pathway to memory and emotion — and yet no one has built a platform that treats it with the depth it deserves.

We believe your fragrance should be as unique as your fingerprint. Not chosen from a shelf. Not recommended by an algorithm that knows nothing about the smell of your grandmother's kitchen or the sound of rain on a cabin roof that makes you feel at home.

Every Zoe fragrance is a genuine Eau de Parfum — 15–18% concentration, crafted by an experienced perfumer, from a creative brief generated by the most detailed preference mapping ever applied to scent. It is real perfumery. It just starts with you instead of a boardroom.

Your scent. Your story. Getting more precise with every wear.
