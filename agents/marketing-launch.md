## Brand Voice Rules

DO:
- Use specific details (name molecules, reference real fragrances)
- Write in second person ("your scent", "your story")
- Be direct and confident
- Use short sentences

DON'T:
- Use generic luxury language ("indulge", "pamper", "luxury experience")
- Use exclamation marks
- Promise perfection — promise iteration
- Use emojis in long-form content
- Say "bespoke" more than once per piece
